Introduction:

Koumei Cottage(KMC)is a decentralized prediction market developed on Asch platform.It belongs to type 1 dapp.And KMC has the features of open-source, autonomy,distributed storage,token incentive,publicity and transparency according to the dapp definition in Asch white-paper(1). The difference from ETH dapp is that its data is stored in the independent block chain, rather than sharing a chain or a set of account book with other application,and is not attached to other block chain system.
